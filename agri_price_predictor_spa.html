About AgriPredict

AgriPredict is dedicated to empowering the agricultural community with cutting-edge technology. Our platform leverages advanced machine learning models to provide accurate price predictions for various crops across different regions, helping farmers and stakeholders make informed decisions, optimize their sales, and enhance profitability. We believe in a future where data-driven insights lead to sustainable and prosperous farming.

Our model considers crucial factors like rainfall, temperature, humidity, pesticide usage, and market arrival to deliver reliable forecasts. We are committed to continuous improvement and expanding our capabilities to serve the agricultural sector better.
